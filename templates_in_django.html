Using Templates In Django

When you are working on a project we need to show the UI using the template and it could be cumbersome for a Data Scientist to work upon templates. You may feel like you are wasting your time instead of improving the accuracy of the algorithm for better results.



Here are the three things you can do to avoid these situations:

1. Try hiring a web designer and ask him to embed the UI into the Django framework. Tell him to leave the editable where your required output will be shown.

2. Try embedding free templates instead of designing or working on the frontend. Try not to dit the template use it as it is. You can choose the template on the basis of the type of output you are required to show on the page.

3. Try asking another developer to help you with the templates and embedding them correctly to the Django Framework. He will do it from the perspective of being developer-friendly. And you can give your time to algorithms.

It’s said “Jack of all trade and Master of none”. So try to outsource the work that you are not comfortable with. And focus on the things that really matter.
